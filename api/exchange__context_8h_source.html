29 /// @brief The ExchangeContext is designed to provide an ease-of-use interface
30 /// for querying and reaggregating information regarding requests and bids of a
31 /// resource exchange.
32 ///
33 /// The ExchangeContext is used by a ResourceExchange or related class to
34 /// provide introspection into the requests and bids it collects. Specifically,
35 /// this class is designed to assist in phases of the Dynamic Resource
36 /// Exchange. The second phase, Response to Request for Bids, is assisted by
37 /// grouping requests by commodity type. The third phase, preference adjustment,
38 /// is assisted by grouping bids by the requester being responded to.
