00021 /// ResMap container for the management of resources. It allows you to associate
00022 /// keys with individual resources. The keys are often strings or ints and the
00023 /// ResMap enables you to add whatever semantic meaning that you want to these keys.
00024 ///
00025 /// Typically, a ResMap will be a member variable of an archetype class.
